Our story

The motivation to start CamEduConsulting came from our own experiences as students starting from scratch in the year 2007, with nothing but lofty ambitions to study abroad. We successfully navigated the difficult application process and got into masters programs in Belgium, Germany, France and The Netherlands. Upon completing our respective degree programs embarking on careers here in Europe, we had a vision to help other Cameroonian students with their study abroad plans and career planning.

We have been operating as educational consultants since 2010, assisting Cameroonian students in furthering their education and realizing their career goals. So far we have recruited students for european universities only. However in the coming years, we plan to also recruit students for other parts of the world.

We work closely with prospective students and help them choose a suitable study program. We introduce them to scholarships and grants geared towards developing countries. We are always looking out for exciting opportunities unknown to most applicants. Aiming to increase the number of admissions from Cameroon, we believe in creating as many national talents as possible. Introducing them to new horizons, diversifying their ideas and skills which are necessary in shaping Cameroon into a prosperous nation in the near future.
