What problems do Cameroonian students wishing to study in Europe face?

Universities in Europe routinely struggle with attracting and selecting suitably qualified international students for their study programs. Everyone knows about the famous universities in the UK; those universities don't struggle with attracting students. They receive thousands of applications from very desperate students who compete for very limited places. Additionally these universities charge crazy tuition fees; we can't count the number of times we have come across students who have been granted an admission but can't pay the tuition fees. Meanwhile, there are hundreds of interesting degree programs offered by universities all over Europe. These degree programs and the exciting career options they open up are unknown to a large number of prospective applicants. Those are the universities we partner with - currently we work with universities in Belgium, Germany, Portugal, Czech Republic and Lithuania. The feedback we have gotten son far suggests that about 70% of applicants from Cameroon send in incomplete applications for admission. Furthermore, from our experience, those who manage to file their applications for admission correctly usually fail to present themselves in the best light possible. This is where we come in - we specialize in bringing European universities and Cameroonian students closer. You can find out more about our story here.

How do we help Cameroonian students planning to study in Europe?

For universities looking to recruit students for their study programs, we connect you with a pool of attractive and eligible candidates from Cameroon. Contact us to start a discussion, we work with you to tailor your course offering to the specifics of the Cameroonian context.

For students looking to study in Europe, we assist you throughout the application process and also offer complementary services. Our process flow consists of the following steps:

  • Examine and propose options for your application file
  • Compose a complete application package
  • Develop a personalized study plan
  • Provide solutions for your application accessories: bank statement, University application fees, affidavit of support, visa application support ...
  • Application follow up
