A little about us

Most Universities in Europe and North America struggle with attracting and selecting suitably qualified international students for their study programs. There are hundreds of interesting degree programs with exciting career options which are unknown to a large number of prospective applicants. This is especially true for African students. Typically 70% of applicants from Africa send in incomplete applications for admission. Furthermore, from our experience, those who manage to file their applications for admission correctly usually fail to present themselves in the best light possible. This is where we come in - we specialize in assisting Universities and students in addressing these problems.

What can we do for you?

For universities looking to recruit students for their study programs, we connect you with a pool of attractive and eligible candidates from Cameroon. Contact us to start a discussion, we work with you to tailor your course offering to the specifics of the Cameroonian context.

For students looking to study abroad, we assist you throughout the application process and also offer complementary services. Typically our process flow consists of the following steps:

  • Examine and propose options to your application file
  • Compose a complete application package
  • Develop a personalized study plan
  • Provide solutions for your application accessories: bank statement, University application fees ...
  • Application follow up
