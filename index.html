A little about us

Universities in Europe and North America face problems when it comes to selecting suitably qualified international students for their study programs. Typically 70% of applicants from Africa send in incomplete applications. Furthermore, from our experience, those who manage to file their applications for admission correctly usually fail to present themselves in the best light possible. Interestingly, there are hundreds of interesting degree programs with exciting career options which are unknown to a large number of prospective applicants. This is where we come in - we specialize in assisting Universities and students in addressing these problems.

What can we do for you?

In addition to assisting you throughout the application processes, we also offer complementary services. Typically our process flow includes:

  • Examine and propose options to your application file
  • Compose a complete application package
  • Develop a personalized study plan
  • Provide solutions for your application accessories: bank statement, University application fees ...
  • Application follow up
